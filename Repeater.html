Englewood Amateur Radio Society (EARS)

Repeaters

EARS maintains a club repeater on 146.865/146.265 and has a weekly network session on every Tuesday at 7:15 pm. The net is primarily a social network and is open to any properly licensed ham, member of the club or not, and provides an opportunity to get to know ham operators "in the neighborhood" and to learn and practice typical Ham net operation. The repeater supports Echo Link

Englewood Amateur Radio Society (EARS)

Membership

The club membership is $25 for the year, and $30.00 per year for a family membership. Below is a link to a "fillable pdf file that you can type in your information and either save it as a pdf file to email to the club, or print and hand carry to a meeting.

The club maintains a repeater site, participates in Field Day Events, local and national Contest Events, and participates in local and statewide emergency prepardness and communications support.

Joining is easy, all you have to do is submit a member application.
